About Us

The Christian Missions Charitable Trust (CMCT) was established in 1964 by Dr. Colleen Redit, aiming to support disadvantaged young women in attaining financial independence and self-sufficiency. Since its inception, CMCT has evolved into a comprehensive charitable organization impacting numerous lives. Our initiatives are closely aligned with seven out of the eight Millennium Development Goals.Education and training are integral components of nearly every CMCT initiative. We firmly believe that empowering vulnerable individuals with new skills enables them to break free from poverty and maintain their autonomy. Regardless of caste, creed, or religious affiliation, we extend our services to those in need.CMCT operates independently of government funding, relying primarily on the generosity of individual donors like yourself. Registered under the Tamil Nadu Societies Act, we are committed to serving our beneficiaries with integrity and dedication.

At CMCT, we maintain a commitment to upholding the highest standards of integrity and excellence in our work. We actively encourage our staff to stay informed about industry-recognized best practices, enabling us to serve our beneficiaries with the utmost effectiveness.Efforts are made to keep administrative overheads to a minimum, ensuring that each project receives sufficient financial backing. Our financial transactions undergo regular audits and are readily accessible for review, demonstrating our dedication to transparency and accountability.
